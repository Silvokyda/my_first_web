Facts and questions

Who are the old racers

This is a group of individuals who are dedicated to providing and bringing that historic time when the wheels were just developing. Our history can be your story.

What is required of me to join the race?

With your passion to join the community, one is required to ofcourse have the basic knowledge i.e driving. Those that register succesfully will be given rally-numbers whivh will identify you, your team and your car.

Payment Options

This is an international event and thus provide variety of payment options. These includes: Paypal, Payoneer, visa etc.

Why Old Racers?

We provide a platform where you can showcase your vintage cars. In addition to that, to the rally lovers you are given a chance to put your engines on full nitrous as you head to the finish line. Winners ofcource are awarded. The awards rage from a free old-racer annual insuarance to a guaranteed 6-month free full service on your machines. Cash awards worth $10,000 will also be given. Join us today and enjoy that nostalgic experience
